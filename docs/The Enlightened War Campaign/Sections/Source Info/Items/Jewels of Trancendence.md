Several Jewels that speak mysterious wonders but give great powers to those who wield

They are connections to this realm held by the [[Council of Malice]]

## Obsidian Gem
The first is the obsidian gem by [[Tiamat]] which takes blood as a sacrifice and bestows purging powers, and powers of excorsism
 - Basically can condem individuals down to the abyss
After a certain amount of sacrifices, one can call upon the power in battle, but then imbues exhaustion


## Moonlight Gem
Has ressurection powers to the dead by the [[Kelevmor]] this stone can instantly revive/stabalize someone at the cost of someone else in the party dropping to 1 hp


## Blaze Gem
Powers of Fire and torment throught [[Asmodeus]]. Gem that lets you sacrifice 2x hp for extra damage

## Reaper Gem
Powers of undead ressurection through [[Kierahnsalee]]
