### Grimoire of Everything
- Sacrifice two of your highest level spell-slots to cast a random spell from a list from the next two highest spell lists

### Ball of Both Sides
- An orb that will follow the chosen person around and argue against every move they make in combat/a scenario for an hour. If the player succesfully argues their action, they will gain advantage on their move roll. If the player does not sufficiently defend their position, they will have disadvantage on their next move roll

### The Memori Marmot
- Allows travel into the Immemoriam Memori
- Puts one in the right mental space
- But still need to find split in the realms to travel within
	- Current Realm splits in the continent
		- Train in the Mine-Metro System
		- The Forgotten Forest
		- The Misty Plains
