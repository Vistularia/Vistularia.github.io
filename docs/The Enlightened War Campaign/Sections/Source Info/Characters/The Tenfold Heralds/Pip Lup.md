# Character Name
!**Insert link to picture** 

Pip was one of the legendary three Pirate Lords back in the day.

Along with Chim and Turt, they were one of the few every to make it past the Ader Vortex and see the rest of the Ader Isles. 

Pip became a well know explorer after their troupes disbanded, and he was said to be of the First individuals ever to make it into the Northern Wasteland and make it back. He first discovered Chrysanthis

Turt would go on to become a renowned Chef 

Chim would go on to become the mayor of Portglassia and the director of the Southeast Crimson Crime Guild
