# Name of City

Here we finally find ourselves in the city of Portglassia, a coastal town on the southern part of the continent. Portglassia is a moderately large new age town (established after the Great Unification) with a more diverse demographic of the great cities. 

Portglassia is a **major hub of the Adventurer Guild** and a location known for being particularly welcome for new adventurers. This is where your characters will find themselves with their **initial goal to register as adventurers with the guild**
